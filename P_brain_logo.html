the Logo concept

The mark is represented by the image of a brain seen from above showing the two hemispheres. The characteristics of each hemisphere are invoked and following Aristotle's famous maxim

“The whole is greater than the sum of its parts”


adapts to the logic of the business as a whole. The lines joining the dots represent the synapses in analogy to predictive intelligence, which is based on analytical processes only possible by correctly linking all available data.
It belongs to the category of “iconic / pictorial mark” as it is an easily recognizable figure (in this case a brain) that has been simplified and stylized.

The logo is represented by the font Vinci Sans.
