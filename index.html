About ME

I am a design thinker,
a creative mind that likes to simplify life.

Based on this mission I’m committed to evolving digital products to the simplest human-centered interaction.

For more than 20 years I have been focused on creating great user experiences in several different ways, which took me from analogic graphic design to digital product design.

Having a passion for great visual design approaches, in each new project, I like to apply Design Thinking methodologies, understand the problem involving the client, and all the stakeholders in a continuous collaboration workflow.

I am a Design strategist, experienced in Design Ops, being able to develop fluid workflow processes, always considering the team as a whole and each individual as a world. Used to work with cross-functional teams making the bridge between designers, developers, and all stakeholders in context, acting many times as a facilitator with the ability to clearly and effectively communicate design processes, ideas, and solutions.

I worked for a long time in R&D teams as principal designer on OTT solutions for TV boxes (Microsoft and later boxes entirely made in-house like the "Power Box"). I collaborated closely with the Software engineers researching and developing new innovative ways of watching TV.

I consider, we have in those days, created a new paradigm for this kind of TV user experience, building some of the first solutions for interactive TV. Designing products for TV boxes gave me the chance to work closely with and for some of the main broadcast channels in several countries like Portugal (NOS, Vodafone, AR Telecom) Spain (Euskaltel), Germany (ISH), South America (Telefónica), designing EPG's, TV Apps for Video on Demand, Live events, Soccer competitions like the world and European cups, UEFA Champions League, Apps for TV shows, etc.

In terms of my personal work process, I am used to dealing with the conception of digital solutions from end-to-end, facing all the product design stages, from sketching to user journeys, wireframing, prototyping mockups, and user-testing in a user-centered design approach focused on creating intuitive user experiences.

Having experience in front-end development, I am fluent in HTML and CSS code, and this background know-how facilitates the way I think and interact with the developers giving me an understanding vision of their point of view, which I try to reflect on the way I build the specs for handoff.

Nowadays I have been focused on Design Systems, being a design System evangelist and working as a Design consultant, helping companies to be on the edge of the digital transformation path.

As a Design system manager I have the whole vision and experience in building a system from scratch, able to structure and define the best practices, design shareable components, the Devs handoff specs, the collaboration workflows, the maintenance processes in an evolving and scalable way according with the companies goals and there digital maturity.

Always keeping my product designer mindset, and loving to break down complex problems into simple solutions, so life can become easier.
