The final result

Video surveillance PLATFORM

The video surveillance market is growing, and there is a huge demand for accurate results.

Until now the surveillance has been made by humans looking to the cameras. The problem is that we humans can't stand being eight hours looking to each detail in all cameras. With human behavior detection, we can create an automated process that will identify the real important actions taking place.


So with this accurate process plus a good user experience we created a successful solution.
